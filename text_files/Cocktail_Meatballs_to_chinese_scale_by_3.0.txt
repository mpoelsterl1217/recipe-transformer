
The ingredients list:
 3 fermented tofu
 1.5 cup bread crumbs
 9 tablespoons minced garlic chives
 6 tablespoons water
 3 large egg
 3 can cranberry sauce
 2.25 cup chili sauce
 3 tablespoon brown sugar
 4.5 teaspoons lemon juice

Steps:
 0. Gather the ingredients.
 1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
 2. Mix ground beef, bread crumbs, garlic chives, water, and egg together in a large bowl.
 3. Form into small meatballs and arrange on a nonstick baking sheet.
 4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, turning once.
 5. Stir cranberry sauce, chili sauce, brown sugar, and lemon juice together in a large saucepan over low heat until smooth.
 6. Add meatballs
 7. simmer for 1 hour before serving.
