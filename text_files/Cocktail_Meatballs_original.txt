
The ingredients list:
 1 pound lean ground beef
 1/2 cup bread crumbs
 3 tablespoons minced onion
 2 tablespoons water
 1 large egg
 1 can cranberry sauce
 3/4 cup chili sauce
 1 tablespoon brown sugar
 1 Â½ teaspoons lemon juice

Steps:
 1. Gather the ingredients.
 2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
 3. Mix ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, water, and egg together in a large bowl.
 4. Form into small meatballs and arrange on a nonstick baking sheet.
 5. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, turning once.
 6. Stir cranberry sauce, chili sauce, brown sugar, and lemon juice together in a large saucepan over low heat until smooth.
 7. Add meatballs
 8. simmer for 1 hour before serving.
