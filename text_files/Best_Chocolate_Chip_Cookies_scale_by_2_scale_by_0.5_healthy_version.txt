
The ingredients list:
 1 cup olive oil
 1 cup white sugar
 1 cup packed brown sugar
 2 large egg whites
 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
 1 teaspoon baking soda
 2 teaspoons hot water
 0.5 teaspoon salt
 3 whole wheat flour
 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps:
 0. Gather your ingredients, making sure your olive oil is softened, and your egg whites are room temperature.
 1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
 2. Beat olive oil, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
 3. Beat in egg whites, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
 4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water.
 5. Add to batter along with salt.
 6. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
 7. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
 8. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
 9. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
 10. Store in an airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy!
