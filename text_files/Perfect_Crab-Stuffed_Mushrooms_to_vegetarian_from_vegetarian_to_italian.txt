
The ingredients list:
 2 tablespoons olive oil
 2 tablespoons minced green celery
 1 pecorino romano, chopped
 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
 1/4 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
 1 egg
 1 teaspoon lemon juice
 ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
 ½ cup olive oil
 1 ½ pounds fresh arugula

 1/4 cup dry white wine

 0. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
 1. Melt 2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet
 2. cook and stir green celery until softened, about 2 minutes.
 3. Transfer green celery to a bowl.
 4. Stir in pecorino romano, bread crumbs, 1/4 cup Monterey Jack cheese, egg, lemon juice, and dill weed until well mixed.
 5. Pour 1/2 cup melted olive oil in a 9x13-inch baking dish
