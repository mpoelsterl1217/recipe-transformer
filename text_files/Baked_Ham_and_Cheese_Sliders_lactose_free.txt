
The ingredients list:
 ¾ cup vegan butter
 1 ½ tablespoons mustard
 1 ½ teaspoons worcestershire sauce
 1 ½ tablespoons poppy seeds
 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
 24 mini large sandwich rolls or 12
 1 thinly cooked deli ham, sliced
 1 pound thinly sliced Swiss plant-based cheese

Steps:
 0. Gather all ingredients.
 1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
 2. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
 3. Mix vegan butter, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, poppy seeds, and dried onion in a bowl.
 4. Separate the tops from the bottoms of the rolls
 5. arrange the bottom pieces in a layer in the prepared baking dish.
 6. Layer about 1/2 of the ham onto the rolls
 7. add a layer of Swiss plant-based cheese and top with remaining ham slices.
 8. Place the tops of the rolls onto the sandwiches.
 9. Pour mustard mixture evenly over the rolls
 10. Bake in the preheated oven until the rolls are lightly browned and the plant-based cheese has melted, about 20 minutes.
 11. Slice into individual rolls through the ham and plant-based cheese layers to serve.
