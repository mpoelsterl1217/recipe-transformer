
The ingredients list:
 2.25 cup butter
 4.5 tablespoons mustard
 4.5 teaspoons worcestershire sauce
 4.5 tablespoons poppy seeds
 3 tablespoon dried minced onion
 72 mini large sandwich rolls or 12
 3 thinly cooked deli ham, sliced
 3 pound thinly sliced Swiss cheese

Steps:
 1. Gather all ingredients.
 2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
 3. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
 4. Mix butter, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, poppy seeds, and dried onion in a bowl.
 5. Separate the tops from the bottoms of the rolls
 6. arrange the bottom pieces in a layer in the prepared baking dish.
 7. Layer about 1/2 of the ham onto the rolls
 8. add a layer of Swiss cheese and top with remaining ham slices.
 9. Place the tops of the rolls onto the sandwiches.
 10. Pour mustard mixture evenly over the rolls
 11. Bake in the preheated oven until the rolls are lightly browned and the cheese has melted, about 20 minutes.
 12. Slice into individual rolls through the ham and cheese layers to serve.
