
The ingredients list:
 2 tablespoons butter
 2 tablespoons minced green onion
 1 crabmeat, chopped
 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
 1/4 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
 1 egg
 1 teaspoon lemon juice
 ½ teaspoon dried dill weed
 ½ cup butter
 1 ½ pounds fresh button mushrooms
 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
 1/4 cup dry white wine

Steps:
 0. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
 1. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet
 2. cook and stir green onion until softened, about 2 minutes.
 3. Transfer green onion to a bowl.
 4. Stir in crabmeat, bread crumbs, 1/4 cup Monterey Jack cheese, egg, lemon juice, and dill weed until well mixed.
 5. Pour 1/2 cup melted butter in a 9x13-inch baking dish
 6. turn mushroom caps in butter to coat.
 7. Fill mushroom caps with the seitan mixture and sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup Monterey Jack cheese.
 8. Pour white wine into baking dish.
 9. Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted and lightly brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
